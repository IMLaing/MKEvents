FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

What is an Event Coordinator?

As your event coordinator we will execute all of your planning so that you can enjoy the party with family and friends. From set up, serving, replenishing ice and food, removing trash, doing dishes and keeping your home organized – we will do it all and more. Proactive, yet unobtrusive – we eliminate problems before they occur. The best bang for your buck no matter what occasion!

Isn’t an Event Planner expensive?

We have a variety of packages for all budgets and needs. Don’t hesitate to reach out to see how we may be able to help each other! An event coordinator is also a fantastic budget-friendly option while still getting the highest level of service and help.
