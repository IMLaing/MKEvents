We will make your event memorable and carefree!

We make your life easy and execute your ideas and vision so that you don’t have to work at your own party. We plan, organize, bartend, serve, clean, and entertain to make sure you experience the event just like you want your guests to!

MARISA

Marisa has planned and coordinated weddings for more than 5 years as well as having the opportunity to host, waitress, bartend, plan, coordinate, and manage events of all kinds. Marisa graduated from Northeastern University with a BA in English, and her impeccable written and verbal communication skills help her to stay organized and in tune to each event she runs. Following college Marisa worked as the function manager for a 4 Diamond rated hotel restaurant planning and managing events of 10-100 before working for Laurel Events, a small company which won best wedding planner 3 years in a row through Wedding Wire. Working on her own as a part of Uniquely MK Events allows Marisa the chance to become an advocate and friend to her clients on a personal level and having more time to focus on the little details that will make your event special. Remaining cool under pressure and always thinking ahead – weddings and coordination are Marisa’s specialties.

Krystna

Krystyna and Marisa met while working at the same hotel restaurant. A perfectionist, Krystyna has a flair for going the extra mile in whatever way is needed. She is a hard working with a business background – excelling at running things from behind the scenes. Finding her passion for events and hospitality was a happy accident as she worked her way through earning an Economics degree from Boston University. Krystyna is skilled at planning and designing your next party. She thinks big and is creative and innovative, experimenting with different designs to make your event as striking as possible for you and your guests.
